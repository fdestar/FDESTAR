WHAT IS FDE?

The Engineers Who
Bridge the Gap

A Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE) is a hybrid technologist who works on-site with clients to deploy AI solutions into real-world environments. Born at Palantir circa 2003, the FDE model has become the dominant approach for enterprise AI adoption.

As MIT research shows, 95% of enterprise generative AI projects fail to deliver ROI. The 5% that succeed share one trait: deep customization and integration — exactly what FDEs provide.

"An FDE is a technologist posted at a client site who, with the help of the product team, figures out how to deliver something valuable." — Bob McGrew, Former Palantir CTO

In 2025–2026, every major AI player — OpenAI, Anthropic, Microsoft, Amazon — has invested billions into building FDE teams. This role is now the fastest-growing position in the AI industry.

HOW WE OPERATE

Organization Structure

Inspired by Palantir's Echo-Delta model, FDE STAR operates through specialized teams working in concert.

  • ECHO UNITS — Needs Discovery

    Deep-dive into client environments, map pain points, and translate real-world challenges into actionable technical requirements. Echo members are the "bilingual" bridge between business and engineering.

  • DELTA UNITS — Build & Deploy

    Rapid prototyping and deployment squads. Delta engineers work hands-on with AI models, pipelines, and infrastructure — adapting solutions to client-specific data, compliance, and workflow constraints.

  • KNOW UNITS — Knowledge Engine

    The institutional memory. Know units systematically capture learnings from every deployment, building reusable playbooks, case studies, and modular solution components that accelerate future projects.

  • TRACKS — Vertical & Skill Lanes

    Industry-specific squads (healthcare, finance, manufacturing, education) and skill-based workshops (prompt engineering, RAG architecture, MLOps). Members join tracks based on their interests and career goals.
